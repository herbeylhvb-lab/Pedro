About Pedro Cardenas

Official photo

Pedro E. Cardenas serves the residents of District 4 on the Brownsville City Commission. First elected in June 2021 and re-elected in June 2025, he brings a background in business administration and account management to his work on the Commission.

Pedro holds a Bachelor of Business Administration from Tecnológico de Monterrey (2003). Before entering public service, he built a career in business and account management, experience that shapes his focus on fiscal responsibility and practical results at City Hall.

As Commissioner, Pedro has championed infrastructure investment, including the $14 million reconstruction of Old Highway 77, and has worked to strengthen public safety, support local small businesses, and keep essential services affordable and reliable for District 4 families.

His current term runs through May 2029.
