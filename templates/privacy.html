Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Pedro Cardenas for City Commissioner campaign (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit this website or sign up to receive communications from us.

Information We Collect

When you sign up through our website, we collect:

  • First and last name
  • Mobile phone number
  • Email address
  • ZIP code
  • Timestamp of signup, IP address, and browser information for recordkeeping
  • The exact consent disclosure shown to you at the time of signup

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to send you campaign updates, community information, and other communications related to the work of Pedro Cardenas as Brownsville City Commissioner. We may contact you by text message, email, or phone using the contact information you provide.

Your Mobile Information

We do not share your mobile information with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information collected during SMS opt-in, including your mobile number and consent records, is kept confidential and used only to deliver the text message program you signed up for.

Information may be shared with service providers we use to deliver text messages (for example, our SMS platform provider), strictly for the purpose of sending the messages you consented to receive, and subject to their own privacy commitments.

SMS Communications

Text message enrollment is opt-in and entirely voluntary. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message. For help, reply HELP or contact us at [CAMPAIGN EMAIL]. See our SMS Terms for full program details.

Data Retention

We retain signup records, including consent records, for a minimum of four (4) years after you unsubscribe, as required by federal telecommunications regulations. After that period, records may be deleted or anonymized.

Your Rights

You may request access to the information we have about you, ask that it be corrected, or request deletion of your information (subject to our legal recordkeeping obligations) by contacting us at [CAMPAIGN EMAIL].

Children’s Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The “Effective date” above will be updated when changes are made. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
